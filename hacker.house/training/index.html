Trainning

  • Ethical Hacking Professional

    Training

    Course Description

    • Gain a comprehensive understanding of ethical hacking principles, methodologies, and legal considerations.
    • Learn to conduct reconnaissance and gather information about target systems and networks.
    • Develop proficiency in scanning and enumeration techniques to identify vulnerabilities and weaknesses.
    • Learn to exploit common security vulnerabilities, including misconfigurations, weak passwords, and software flaws.
    • Understand the principles of social engineering and how to use it effectively in ethical hacking engagements.
    • Learn to conduct penetration tests, vulnerability assessments, and security audits using industry-standard tools and techniques.
    • Explore advanced topics in ethical hacking, including web application security, wireless network security, and cloud security.
    • Learn to document findings, prepare comprehensive reports, and communicate effectively with stakeholders.
  • Cyber Secure Leadership

    Training

    Course Description

  • Understand the fundamental concepts of cybersecurity and the latest digital threats.
  • Recognize the importance of cybersecurity within the business context and its impact on operations.
  • Learn to identify potential cybersecurity risks and implement effective risk management strategies.
  • Develop an understanding of cybersecurity best practices and how to apply them in daily business operations.
  • Cultivate a cybersecurity-aware culture within the organization.
  • Gain insights into regulatory compliance and legal responsibilities related to cybersecurity.
  • Penetration Testing

    Training

    Course Description

  • Understand the fundamentals of penetration testing methodologies and ethical hacking.
  • Master reconnaissance and information gathering techniques to gather intelligence.
  • Learn scanning and enumeration methodologies to identify vulnerabilities.
  • Gain expertise in exploiting vulnerabilities and post-exploitation techniques.
  • Conduct thorough web application penetration tests to identify security flaws.
  • Perform network penetration tests to assess network security controls.
  • Conduct wireless network penetration tests to identify wireless security vulnerabilities.
  • Understand social engineering and phishing attacks and how to mitigate them.
  • Develop skills in report writing and documentation for communicating findings and recommendations.
  • Familiarize yourself with essential penetration testing tools and their applications.
  • Red Team Operations

    Training

    Course Description

  • Deepen understanding of red team methodologies and frameworks (MITRE ATT&CK, CARVER, Diamond Model).
  • Develop advanced skills in planning, executing, and reporting on red team engagements.
  • Learn how to effectively bypass security controls and achieve objectives.
  • Prepare students for real-world red teaming challenges and exercises.
  • Ethical Hacking Essentials

  • Understand the ethical and legal implications of hacking.
  • Learn the fundamentals of information security and cyber threats.
  • Gain basic knowledge in assessing and improving the security posture of systems.
  • Prepare for advanced training and certifications in ethical hacking.
